About Me

I imagine worlds and use AI to make them real

I'm Tharindu Athapaththu, an imaginor and creator based in Colombo, Sri Lanka. I see vivid worlds in my mind: ancient romances, neon-lit fantasies, culturally rich cinematic stories. Then I use AI tools to bring them to life. From image generation to video, music, and post-production, every tool is a brush that turns imagination into reality.

With a background in Software Engineering at Arimac Digital, I bring systematic precision to every creative project. I treat prompts like screenplays and iterations like refinement passes. The result is AI-crafted content that rivals traditional production quality, powered not just by technology, but by vision.

Creative Philosophy

Prompt as Screenplay

Every prompt is a scene direction. I treat AI generation not as random output, but as a collaborative filmmaking process where language precision determines visual excellence.

Iteration is Everything

The first generation is never the final one. Through systematic refinement (adjusting lighting, camera angles, expressions, and cultural details) each image evolves toward cinematic quality.

Cultural Authenticity

Every project is rooted in genuine cultural research. From Japanese Edo-period architecture to Puerto Rican traditional dress, accuracy of detail elevates AI art beyond surface aesthetics.
